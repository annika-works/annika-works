School's Out Poster

In the height of the Berlin heatwave '18, my band played a gig at a local cultural centre. This is the poster I illustrated for the occasion. The gig's theme was 'School's Out' - quite blantly, given that it was in the middle of Berlin school holidays. I went for a playful usage of colours, a quirky font and illustrative elements for that last days of (youthful) summer feeling.
