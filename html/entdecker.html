Der kleine Entdecker

Der kleine Entdecker (The little Adventurer) is a brochure published by Children for a better World e.V., an NGO working towards better opportunities for children growing up in socially deprived families. In order to communicate their work and help institutions and parents to get children out and play, they put together this helpful guide. I was lucky enough to take over the design part: I put up a layout, designed each page and prepared it for print.
