Fux Bier

In Musberg near Stuttgart, Benjamin 'Fuchs' Braun brews his Fux Bier. In my time at MüllerValentini Agency, I worked on making his visual identity a reality - and crafted the logo of the now-on-sale beer. Svenja Prigge took care of the characteristic font, I illustrated the name-giving fox, MüllerValentini were responsible for the Art Dircetion. Cheers! www.fux-bier.de
